...here. Still.

Introduction to "...here. Still."

"...here. Still." is an interactive fiction game, set in a small and quiet village.

You will experience a 7-day journey, interacting with four villagers there: builder, farmer, fisher, and woodcutter.

Every day, you will choose whether to help the villagers, persuade them to skip work, or just leave them alone silently.

You will decide how your journey goes, and what kind of change you leave behind.

Play Instructions

Moving the character: WASD or arrow keys

Triggering interaction with characters: F

Progressing dialogue: SPACE key

Choosing dialogue options: 1, 2, 3
